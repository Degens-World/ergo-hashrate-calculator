💡 Mining Tips

🔋

Optimize Power

Undervolting your GPU can reduce power by 20-30% with minimal hashrate loss, significantly improving profitability.

🌡️

Temperature Matters

Keep GPU temps below 70°C for Ergo mining. Autolykos v2 is memory-hard — hot VRAM degrades performance.

🏊

Choose Your Pool

Popular Ergo pools: Enigma Pool, WoolyPooly, HeroMiners. Look for low fees (1%) and PPLNS payout schemes.

📦

HODL Strategy

Mining ERG and holding long-term has historically outperformed selling immediately. Consider your time horizon.
